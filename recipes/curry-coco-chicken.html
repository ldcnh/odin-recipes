Curry Chicken and Coco

This curry chicken is rich, creamy and delicious. It is great over thai rice!

Ingredients

  • 600 g of chicken breast
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 1 onion
  • 200 ml of coco milk
  • 2 tbs of curry powder
  • 2 tbs of olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion to the skillet and cook until it begins to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with curry powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the spices.
  4. Add the sliced bell pepper and zucchini to the skillet. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the chicken to the skillet, then add the coconut milk. Stir well to combine, then bring the mixture to a simmer.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the skillet, and let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavors are well combined.
