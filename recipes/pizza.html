Margherita Pizza

This pizza is a versatile and popular food that can be customized with a wide variety of toppings to suit individual tastes, making it a favorite choice for casual meals and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of mozarella cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbs of olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting, usually around 475-500°F (245-260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. If you like a thin crust, roll it out thinner, or leave it a bit thicker for a more doughy crust.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  4. Add the sliced bell pepper and zucchini to the skillet. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the pizza.
  6. Bake the pizza for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
